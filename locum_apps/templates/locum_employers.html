Choosing Locums Choice

You have a lot of choice in locum tenens staffing. Here are three reasons to work with Locums Choice.

Staffing Know-How

We know locums tenens. From leadership to delivery, our team is comprised of individuals who have committed their careers to medical staffing. When you talk to us, you’re talking to peers who understand your challenges, your jargon, and your specific hiring requirements. Our knowledge helps us work as a true partner to you in supporting your staffing goals.

Delivery Expertise

Providers need to be licensed, credentialed and onsite and ready to work when you need them. We have trained delivery professionals at every step of the process to take care of all the details—so you don’t have any surprises.

Quality Providers

We provide A++ occurrence malpractice insurance through Medical Protective to recruit and retain top providers and protect our clients. MedPro individually underwrites and approves each provider, and we credential our locum tenens following the standards of the National Association of Locum Tenens Organizations (NALTO). We conduct regular quality surveys to ensure your satisfaction and we’ll replace any provider that doesn’t meet your standards.
