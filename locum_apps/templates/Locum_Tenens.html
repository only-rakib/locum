Work in multiple states with IMLC

Ask our recruiters if you qualify for the Interstate Medical Licensure Compact (IMLC). The Compact aims to relieve physician shortages by making it easier for qualified physicians to practice in multiple states, if they meet agreed upon eligibility requirements.

The Application process uses your existing information submitted in your state of principal license (SPL). The SPL will verify your information and conduct a fresh background check. Once qualified you may select any number of Compact states in which to practice.
